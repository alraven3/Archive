Public Surveys On Security & City

Focusing on collaborations between the Edgelands Institute and local partners in Medellín, Geneva, and Nairobi, the following reports and surveys document in-person events, debates, and interviews with citizens in each of these cities, showcasing their perspectives on security, technology, and the urban experience. Aside from giving the opportunity for the public to express their views on these important issues, the collected surveys also provided invaluable data for researchers and private organisations. The results from these surveys provide significant insight into citizen experience of security and a solid foundation for future research in this area.
