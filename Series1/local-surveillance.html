Local Surveillance and Security Analysis

The following reports and videos document the Edgelands Institute fellowship led in Medellín, Colombia, in 2023. The research conducted by fellows focused on the question of video surveillance and digital security in the city, resulting in detailed data reports and analyses of the effectiveness, public reception, and possible drawbacks of these security strategies. Accompanied by visual graphics and other data, these reports provide crucial insight into the security concerns encountered by the population and the strategies that can be implemented to improve these issues and to strengthen public co-existence.
