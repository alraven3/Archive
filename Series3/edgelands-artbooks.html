Artbooks

Throughout the collaborative endeavour of the Edgelands Institute, art played a central role. It does not merely illustrate, rather it actively participates in research. The vast production of artwork (photography, videos, performances, installations, painting, among others) led to the publication project of three Artbooks. The aim was to create a narrative specific to Edgelands, to create meaning and raise questions around the theme of the digitalisation of security and its impact on the social fabric of cities, through philosophical questioning, as well as poetic and graphic gestures.
