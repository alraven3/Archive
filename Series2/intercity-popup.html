Intercity Digital Pop-Up

Through a series of intercity digital events, the Edgelands Institute brought together its network from its five city partners (Medellín, Cúcuta, Geneva, Nairobi and Houston). This was an opportunity to further develop a dialogue between the different stakeholders and to exchange experiences, observations and solutions on issues relating to digital integrity. Three specific dimensions were explored during these Intercity pop-ups: urban security, safety and trust in the digital space, as well as security and physical control of infrastructure and data. The aim of these Intercity pop-up discussions was to learn from each other’s experiences on a local level and to foster dialogue and exchange on a global level, in the hope of building a collective understanding of this crucial issue. This dynamic exchange is documented in the following blogposts, videos and reports.
